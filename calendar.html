Calendar

For instant updates regarding all things McKinney Boyd High School, be sure to take a look at our social media.

Parents and students are encouraged to add the calendar above to their personal devices through our calendar's URL, provided below.

For Apple devices, go to Settings > Mail, Contacts, Calendars > Add Account > Other > Add Subscribed Calendar

Type in the URL: bit.ly/MBHScalendar (be sure to use caps where indicated)

And then save.

You should be able to see the school calendar on your iOS device without any problem.
